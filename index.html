How it works ?

Coop Search

  1. Create your Ulink profile with your program, year of study, skills and interests.
  2. uLink automatically scans verified co-op and internship postings from partner companies and institutions.
  3. Our matching system filters opportunities that align with your program and experience level.
  4. View all relevant openings in a single dashboard — with details like duration, location, and eligibility.
  5. Apply directly through uLink using your saved profile and resume — no repetitive forms needed.
  6. Track your applications and receive status updates in real time.
  7. Get notified instantly when new matching co-op opportunities are posted.

Scholarship Search

  1. Set up your academic profile — including GPA, program, nationality, and financial needs.
  2. uLink compiles available scholarships from universities, government.
  3. The recommendation system filters and displays only scholarships that match your eligibility criteria.
  4. Access key information instantly — amount, deadline, and required documents.
  5. Apply directly through uLink or access the official application page with pre-filled details.
  6. Receive reminders before deadlines and updates on your submitted applications.
  7. Save and organize your favorite scholarships for later review.
